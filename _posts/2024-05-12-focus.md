Steps to compile the FOCUS code.

## Requirements for compiler:
- c++ (gcc>=9.3)
- nvcc (for gpu)

## Requirements for libraries:
- openmpi
- boost
- extlibs/gsl
- extlibs/magma (for gpu)
- extlibs/nccl (for gpu)
- extlibs/zquatev
- src/ctns/gpu_kernel
